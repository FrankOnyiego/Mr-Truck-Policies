Privacy Policy — Mr Truck

Effective Date: December 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how BSC Limited (“Company”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal data when you use the Mr Truck mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

We comply with Kenya’s Data Protection Act (2019) and related regulations.

2. Data Controller & Contact

3. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data when you use the Service:

  • Identity Information: name, national ID/passport, date of birth.
  • Contact Information: phone number, email, address.
  • Location Data: GPS pickup and drop-off locations for service delivery.
  • Payment Data: card/payment details (processed via third-party providers), transaction history.
  • Device & Usage Data: device identifiers, IP address, app usage logs, crash reports.
  • Communications: messages or calls between Customers and Drivers (where retained for support and service quality).
  • Optional Data: photos or documents uploaded for proof of delivery or ID verification.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Performance of a contract – to provide transport and delivery services.
  • Consent – where required (e.g., marketing communications).
  • Legal obligations – to comply with Kenyan laws.
  • Legitimate interests – fraud prevention, service improvement, dispute resolution.

5. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Service.
  • To process payments and manage bookings.
  • To enable live tracking and route optimisation.
  • To verify identity and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • To conduct analytics and improve customer experience.

6. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Drivers – to fulfil your booking.
  • Payment processors – to complete transactions.
  • Service providers – such as hosting, analytics, and customer support providers.
  • Law enforcement or regulators – if required by law.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Cross-Border Transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside Kenya, we ensure compliance with the Data Protection Act (2019) and related regulations on data localisation and cross-border transfers.

8. Data Security & Retention

We use encryption, access controls, and other technical safeguards to protect your data. We retain your data only as long as necessary to deliver the Service, comply with laws, or for legitimate business purposes.

9. Your Rights

Under Kenyan law, you have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal data.
  • Correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion of data (subject to legal limitations).
  • Restriction or objection to processing.
  • Data portability (where applicable).

You may also file a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC).

10. Children’s Data

Mr Truck is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
