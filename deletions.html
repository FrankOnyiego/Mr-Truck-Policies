Data That Will Be Deleted

Once your deletion request is verified, the following data will be permanently deleted from our systems:

  • Personal account details (name, email, phone number)
  • Profile and login credentials
  • Trip and delivery records linked to your user account
  • Payment information (if applicable)

Data That May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal or business purposes, including:

  • Transaction or invoice records required by tax or transport regulations
  • Security logs necessary to detect and prevent fraud
Retained data is stored securely and deleted after the legally required retention period (typically up to 6 months).
