Refunds Policy — Mr Truck

Effective Date: September 2025

1. Scope

This Refunds Policy applies to all payments made through the Mr Truck mobile application for transportation and logistics services. It explains the conditions under which refunds may be approved, how refund requests are processed, and your statutory rights as a consumer under Kenyan law.

2. Eligible Grounds for Refunds

You may be eligible for a full or partial refund under the following circumstances:

  • Service not provided – If a Driver fails to arrive or does not complete the agreed service.
  • Duplicate charge or payment error – If you were charged twice or experienced a technical payment issue.
  • Materially different service – If the service delivered is substantially different from what was agreed.
  • Damaged goods (where applicable) – For delivery-related services where goods arrive damaged due to the Driver’s fault (subject to evidence).

3. Exclusions

Refunds will not be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Cancellations made outside the free-cancellation window stated in the app.
  • Change-of-mind after a service has already been completed.
  • Disputes relating to goods transported where the Driver demonstrates that the agreed service was performed correctly.

4. How to Request a Refund

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the booking by either:

  • Using the in-app support feature: Menu → Support → Report a Problem
  • Emailing us at bsclimited.mrtruck@gmail.com with your booking ID, date, and evidence (e.g., screenshots, photos).

5. Review Process

Once a refund request is received:

  • We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours.
  • We may contact you or the Driver to verify details.
  • We aim to complete investigations and provide a resolution within 7–14 business days.

6. Refund Method

If approved, refunds will be processed back to your original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

7. Partial Refunds

Where applicable, partial refunds may be issued for:

  • Late arrival by the Driver
  • Partially completed service
  • Mutually agreed adjustments between Customer and Driver

8. Consumer Rights

This Refunds Policy does not limit your legal rights under the Kenya Consumer Protection Act or other applicable laws. Where statutory rights apply, they will prevail in addition to this Policy.

9. Chargebacks & Disputes

If you dispute a charge with your payment provider, we will cooperate with their investigation. If their decision is in our favour, we reserve the right to recover disputed amounts directly from you.
