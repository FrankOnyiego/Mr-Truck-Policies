Policy — Mr Truck

This Refunds & Cancellation Policy governs refunds for bookings made through the Mr Truck app.

  • Refund Grounds: driver fails to arrive or complete service; duplicate charge; service materially differs; proven driver negligence where insurance applies.
  • Cancellation Policy:
    • On-demand bookings: free cancellation within 10 minutes, after which up to 50% fee applies.
    • Scheduled bookings: free cancellation up to 2 hours before pickup. Within 2 hours, 50–100% fee may apply.
    • No refund once pickup has started (driver en route, goods loaded).
  • Exclusions: no refund for improper packaging, uninsurable goods, or where the customer declined GIT insurance.
  • Request process: submit within 2 business days via in-app support or email bsclimited.mrtruck@gmail.com.
  • Timeline: requests acknowledged within 48 hours, investigated in 7–14 business days. Refunds processed to the original payment method.
